Meet the Creator

Hello, I'm Parker, the creator of PunchPredictor. At 21 years old, as a college student deeply passionate about combat sports, both as a spectator and a practitioner, I envisioned a place where enthusiasts like you and me could predict fight outcomes and track our performance over time without spending money. This website is my passion project, crafted in between classes and training sessions. It's a one-person show here at PunchPredictor, aiming to bring you the best in fight prediction and community engagement. Thank you for being part of this journey!
